    Our Products.

    Vermi Compost

    Enriched compost which is rich in microbial life which converts nutrients already present in the soil into plant-available forms.

    Coirpith Compost

    It is composted to reduce the wider C:N ratio, reduce the lignin and cellulose content and also to increase the manorial value of pith.

    Cow dung Powder

    It makes an excellent growing medium for garden plants. It can be mixed into the soil or used as top dressing.

    Potting Mixture

    Red soil is enriched with 10 kinds of fertilizers and packed in a bag where we can plant vegetables. It is enough for complete growth of a plant.

    Organic Medium

    A semi meal for all kinds of plants, vegetables, fruits and flowers for healthy and clean growth

    Neem Cake

    It protects plant roots from nematodes, soil grubs and termites, probably due to it's residual limonoid content.
