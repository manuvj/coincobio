    What We Offering .

    Are you going to make healthy food out of organic farming ? We may have something to help you.

    Vermi Compost

    It is rich in microbial life which converts nutrients already present in the soil into plant-available forms.

    Coirpith Compost

    It improves the texture, structure and tilth of the soil. Thus helping plants to grow fast and healthy.

    Cow dung Powder

    It makes an excellent growing medium for garden plants. It can be mixed into the soil or used as top dressing.

    Organic Medium

    A semi meal, mixture of vermi compost, coirpith compost, vermiculate, perlite, macro and micro nutrients etc.

    Neem Cake

    It protects plant roots from nematodes, soil grubs and termites, probably due to it's residual limonoid content.

    Coirpith Block

    It is made from coconut husk. It retains moisture, stores and releases nutrients to roots over extended period of time.

    Organic potting mixture

    Red soil is enriched with 10 kind of fertilizers and packed to we can plant vegetables. For complete plant growth.

    Clay Ball

    It prevent the plants from completely drying out but still provide proper drinage, which means less watering. Light weight

    Perlite

    It is used in soil mixes to improve aeration and modify the soil substructure, keeping it loose and defying compaction.

    Vermiculite

    When it's mixed with soil/peat promotes faster root growth and holding strength to young roots. Retains plants food and release when required.

    Panchagavya

    It is a traditional mixture made out of five ingredients (Cow dung, urine, milk, curd and ghee). It can be used as fertilizer and organic pesticide.

    Fish Amino Acid

    Juice of fish is extracted and gets fermented after storing for a period. It enhances the growth of crops during their vegetative period.
