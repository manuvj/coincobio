    What We Offering .

    Are you going to make healthy food out of organic farming ? We may have something to help you.

    Vermi Compost

    Enriched compost which is rich in microbial life which converts nutrients already present in the soil into plant-available forms.

    Coirpith Compost

    It is composted to reduce the wider C:N ratio, reduce the lignin and cellulose content and also to increase the manorial value of pith.

    Cow dung Powder

    It makes an excellent growing medium for garden plants. It can be mixed into the soil or used as top dressing. Used from ancient past.

    Grow Bag

    Red soil is enriched with 10 kind of fertilizers and packed in a bag in which we can plant vegetables. It is enough for complete plant growth.

    Panchagavya

    It is a traditional mixture made out of five ingredients (Cow dung, urine, milk, curd and ghee). It can be used as fertilizer and organic pesticide.

    Fish Amino Acid

    Juice of fish is extracted and gets fermented after storing for a period. It enhances the growth of crops during their vegetative period.
